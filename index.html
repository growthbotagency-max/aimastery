Yes — we've designed this specifically for beginners. Whether you're a student, professional, or complete newcomer to AI, the program starts from the ground up. All concepts are explained in plain language (Hinglish where needed) with live demonstrations.

That is the goal of Modules 7 and 8. We provide done-for-you outreach scripts, proposal templates, and first client closing support. Most students land their first client between weeks 5–8. We cannot guarantee results, but we give you every tool and support needed to make it happen.
How long does the program last?+
The core implementation program runs for 8–10 weeks with weekly live calls. After completion, you get lifetime access to all content, recordings, and the mastermind community. You also receive 3 months of support included in the package.
What is the time commitment per week?+
We recommend 10–15 hours per week for best results — this includes the live call, implementation sessions, and client outreach. If you're working a job, this is absolutely manageable in evenings and weekends. Many of our students built their first client while still employed full-time.
Is there a refund policy?+
We offer a 7-day refund if you complete less than 20% of the material and feel the program isn't right for you. Given this is a high-commitment mentorship, we do ask all applicants to go through a strategy call before enrolling to ensure it's a strong fit.
